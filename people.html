Bernard Wu, Ph.D

I earned my Sc.B and A.B at Brown University and completed my master and Ph.D. at Princeton University. My graduate research was focused on using optical steganography methods to enhance the physical layer security of fiber-optic networks. Early in my career, I worked in multiple investment banks primarily in the equity derivative space. My most recent experience was with UBS in Hong Kong where I worked as an equity derivative trader for ~ 10.5 years.
